title: "Turning Text into Measurable Emotional Signal"
excerpt: "Extracted interpretable, quantitative emotional and structural features from raw text to predict behavioral outcomes, not just sentiment scores."
collection: portfolio
---

Most text analysis stops at positive/negative/neutral. This project goes further: eight emotional dimensions from a psychological framework, plus structural features from treating text as a network of connected words. The task was separating two groups of writers from their text alone (using NLP).

Applies wherever there's open-text data to quantify, survey verbatims, customer feedback, user research transcripts.

A model that predicts too well is often a warning sign, so the project includes a step-by-step check of which features actually drove the result.

**What this shows:** turning raw text (such as user reviews for a product) into quantitative interpretable signal, and validating it before trusting it.
